PongAttack is a modern take on the classic Pong game. It includes enhanced gameplay mechanics, additional features, and an updated design to make the game more engaging and fun.

In this game, you need to click on the arrow keys to move and try to avoid the ping-pong balls. You can press space to activate shield, but it costs 2 points per second. If you get hit by a ball or have less than 0 points, you are out.

The leaderboard shows the top 10 scores.
